Diet Guide by BMI Category

Use these sample meal ideas for underweight, normal, overweight, and obese categories. Calories are approximate and can be adjusted to personal needs.

Underweight

Target: maintenance + 300 to 500 kcal/day
  • Breakfast: banana shake + oats + peanut butter (450–600 kcal)
  • Snack: nuts + dates + milk (250–350 kcal)
  • Lunch: rice + dal + paneer / tofu + curd (600–800 kcal)
  • Dinner: roti + potato sabji + chickpeas + yogurt (500–700 kcal)

Normal

Target: maintenance calories
  • Breakfast: oats / eggs / paneer + fruit (300–450 kcal)
  • Lunch: roti or rice + dal + vegetables + protein (450–650 kcal)
  • Snack: yogurt + fruit or nuts (150–250 kcal)
  • Dinner: balanced meal with vegetables and protein (400–600 kcal)

Overweight

Target: maintenance - 300 to 500 kcal/day
  • Breakfast: oats with fruit or eggs / paneer (250–350 kcal)
  • Snack: apple, orange, cucumber, buttermilk (80–150 kcal)
  • Lunch: salad + dal + 1–2 roti + tofu / paneer (350–500 kcal)
  • Dinner: soup + vegetables + lean protein (250–400 kcal)

Obese

Target: maintenance - 500 to 700 kcal/day
  • Breakfast: vegetable oats / egg whites / paneer (220–320 kcal)
  • Snack: low-sugar fruit or unsweetened yogurt (80–140 kcal)
  • Lunch: large salad + dal + 1 roti + protein (300–450 kcal)
  • Dinner: soup + vegetables + tofu / paneer / lean protein (220–350 kcal)
